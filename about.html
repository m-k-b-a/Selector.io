What's Selector.io About?

Selector.io is more than just a step into the present. Here's why I created Selector.io:

  • I created Selector.io to fuel my passion about video games. Ever since I was little, I love video games and to manage the servers one day is my dream job.
  • This started as a project for my INFO 151 class and I hope I can expand this until a fully functional website that has frequent updates to it.
  • I hope that everyone who finds this website can have fun with this website and allow their love for video games to continue to grow.
